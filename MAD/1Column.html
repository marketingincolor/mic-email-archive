Congratulations you have been selected to take part in Madico’s new Dealer Loyalty Program!

Dear (DEALER NAME),

This new program is designed exclusively for our most successful dealers and is intended to reward you for your loyalty and commitment to growing your business with Madico Window Films.
 
You have been automatically enrolled in the program based on your 2016 film purchase amount of (INSERT $$). The best part of our Loyalty Program is that you do not have to do anything to take part, just continue to purchase Madico products for your business.
 
Here is a breakdown of how the program works: You must have purchased at least $20,000 worth of Madico products in 2016 to qualify for the program. Qualifying purchases include all Madico automotive and architectural products including the SafetyShield, Sunscape, Invisi-Film, MACS, and Sun-Gard brands, as well as literature and tools. If you increase your purchase amount with Madico in 2017, that additional purchase amount will qualify for a 5% rebate. The rebate applies only to the purchases made over the amount of the previous year.
 
For example, if your 2016 purchase amount was $21,000 and your 2017 purchase amount ends up being $31,000. You would receive a 5% rebate ($500) on the $10,000 worth of additional purchases.  The rebate will be issued in the form of a credit applied to your account during the first quarter of 2018.  All accounts must be in good financial standing with Madico to qualify for the rebate.
 
We are looking forward to rewarding your efforts and commitment to Madico through this new Loyalty Program! If you have any questions regarding the Madico Loyalty Program, please contact your local branch or sales representative.
 
Best Regards,
 
Jim Black
Sales Director, Americas
