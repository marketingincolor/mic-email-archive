Did you know that as of 2017, nationally 1 in 3 Medicare beneficiaries now have Medicare Advantage? This number is even higher in the state of Florida where 42% of Medicare beneficiaries have Medicare Advantage.* 

National Trends for Medicare Advantage

Not only are more individuals selecting Medicare Advantage plans, but Group Plan enrollment, comprised largely of plans sponsored by unions and employers for retirees, has doubled since 2008.

In the June edition of Forbes, UnitedHealth® Group's new CEO, Dave Wichmann, was quoted as saying, “Our Medicare Advantage business continues to benefit from strong consumer retention reflecting seniors' positive experience and the clear economic value of our offerings.” Executives at UnitedHealth Group predict 50% of seniors will choose Medicare Advantage over time. Read more.

Learn more about the trends and growth of Medicare Advantage by visiting the Kaiser Family Foundation.

Many studies show patients choosing Medicare Advantage have better health outcomes. We chose to work with Medicare Advantage because it is better for patients and supports our care model focused on prevention and care coordination. In addition to better care, TVH patients often save money selecting a Medicare Advantage plan instead of a Medicare Supplement policy.
 
*June 2017 Report from Kaiser Family Foundation “Medicare Advantage 2017 Spotlight: Enrollment Market Update"

Patient Service Representatives Available During Annual Enrollment Period (AEP)

The Villages Health has a Patient Service Representative (PSR) located in every care center, including our Specialty Care Center and new Brownwood Care Center. If you have clients who are looking for a new health care provider, we welcome the opportunity to give them a tour. Our PSRs will show prospective patients around the care center most convenient to their home, and discuss their physician preferences and any medical needs they may have. 
 
Our PSRs are knowledgeable about how everything works within The Villages Health, and can answer any questions your clients may have about our medical services and becoming a patient. Keep us in mind throughout AEP for any assistance you may need to showcase health care options available to your clients.

To schedule a care center tour with one of our Patient Service Representatives, call 352-674-1700.
